JACK is a game leading players through a thoughtful journey as they travel within a surreal world, exploring philosophical themes and metaphor. Bringing acrylic painting along with pencil drawings to life, the players through Jack - a young boy - encounter the meaning and beauty (and despair) that is life.

After learning of his impending death, a boy ventures into a vibrant surreal world seeking purpose for his very existence.

At its core, JACK is a game following the titular Jack’s exploration of his existence. We come to understand, as he does over the course of the game, that it is not about the destination but rather the journey itself.

"We have two lives, and the second begins when we realize we only have one" Confucius’ philosophical concept
